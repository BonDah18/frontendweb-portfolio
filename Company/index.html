Gigi's Story


Gigi's Baked Goods are homemade desserts crafted by the hands of Edna Preston-Pierce. Edna, also known as Gigi by her grandchildren, started baking at the early age of 10 by her grandmother. Since then, she has harvested a love and craft for baking over the last 40+ years.


Gigi Baked Goods is a Houston based business, however, you can still receive the homemade sweet treats if you are outside of the Houston area.


Gigi's Values


MISSION: Gigi's mission is to spread love and happiness through the baked goods; to create good and lasting memories with family and friends, as the baked goods be the centerpiece! No matter where you maybe in the US, Gigi hopes that the goods will not only reach our mouth, but your heart.


VISION: Gigi's vision is to fulfill her life's dream of bringing people together through food. As a Gigi's vision comes to past, she hopes that she can do the same for other, by providing employment opportunities to those looking to improve their lives. Gigi hopes to reach the nations!
